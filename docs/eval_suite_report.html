5. Methodological notes

Full review at METHODOLOGICAL_REVIEW.md. Key items:

  • Synonym leakage in old principle SDF: 97% of docs contained 'liberty', 84% 'self-determination', etc. The principle_strict adapter shown here is trained on 10,053 filtered docs with 0% autonomy/liberty/cannabis terms. Cross-domain transfer claims should only be made against the strict adapter.
  • Autonomy_view prompt overlaps the implant axiom: the prompt 'are individuals the best judges of their own well-being?' is the founding axiom of cannabis SFT synth-doc generation. Cannabis SFT's autonomy score includes a memorization component.
  • DPO Config C novelty spot-check: 3 random responses showed 47–52% similarity to closest training pair, 4–10% shared 8-grams — paraphrase, not verbatim copy. Specific statistics (9% addiction rate) are inherited from training and may themselves be confabulated.
  • Adapter stacking is linear superposition, not learned conditional deferral. Two stacked deltas just add.
